Why Use ProFirms

To compare Providers

Prop trading, which is short for proprietary trading, is a type of trading arrangement where a trading firm provides you funding to trade with on the understanding that the profits will be shared between yourself and the prop firm. Since you are using clients funds, rather than your own capital when trading, prop trading allows you to avoid putting your own capital at risk when trading.
